Welcome to our Website

The goal of this website is to showcase our personal health record (PHR) system. This PHR system uses a blockchain to store the patients data.

Here is a quick rundown of our websites features:
  • The 'Info' button allows you to view all the blocks in the blockchain.
  • The 'Generate key' button generates a public key and private key and downloads it to clients machine.
  • The 'Add block' button gives the client a form to enter the patients data. Once the data it is used to create a block that will be added to the chain.
