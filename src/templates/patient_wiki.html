Help Page

Welcome to our wiki. Please use the navigation bar on the left side of your screen to quickly navigate to any section.

How to generate a key pair

1. In order to generate your keys, select the "Generate Keys" option from the navigation bar.




2. Once you are on that page, click on the "Generate Keys" button.



3. Your browser will prompt you to save the file somewhere. Navigate to the folder you would like to save the key to, and then save it.



4. Extract the "keys.zip" file that you just downloaded.



5. Your "public_key.pem" file and your "private_key.pem" files should now be available to you. NEVER share your private key with anyone, under any circumstances. If someone gains access to your private key they will permanently be able to decrypt and view all of your records.

Viewing your data (view your blocks)

Before beginning, make sure you have your key files ready.

1. To start with viewing your data, click on "View Records" located on the navigation bar.



2. Under the "Upload Private key" section, click "browse" and locate "private_key.pem".

3. Click the "Upload" button.

4. Under the "Upload Public key" section, click "browse" and locate "public_key.pem".



5. Click the "Upload" button.


6. (OPTIONAL) Fill the "Transaction ID" field with one specific transaction ID in order to view only that. If this is left empty, all of the transaction ID's stored within your provided "public_key.pem" file will be loaded.


7. Click the "View History" button.


8. You should now see a table populated with all of the requested data. You can sort by any column and filter by any field.
